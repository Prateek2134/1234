Prevent Rice plant Disease follow below steps:
  1. Use Healthy Seeds: Start with healthy seeds or seedlings from a reputable source. Healthy seeds have a better chance of resisting diseases.
  2. Crop Rotation: Rotate rice with other crops to break disease cycles and reduce the buildup of pathogens in the soil.
  3. Field Selection: Choose well-drained fields with good air circulation to reduce the risk of disease development.
  4. Weed Control: Keep fields weed-free to reduce competition for nutrients and minimize the spread of diseases carried by weeds.
  5. Sanitation: Remove and destroy infected plant debris to prevent the buildup of pathogens
